# Social Circle - Quick Reference Guide

A quick guide to using all features of your social media platform.

## For All Users

### Creating an Account
1. Click "Sign Up" on the homepage
2. Enter your full name, email, and password (min 6 characters)
3. Optionally enter an invite code if you have one
4. Click "Create Account"

### Logging In
1. Click "Log In" on the homepage
2. Enter your email and password
3. Check "Remember me" to stay logged in on this device
4. Click "Log In"

### Forgot Password?
1. Click "Log In" then "Forgot password?"
2. Enter your email address
3. Click "Send Reset Link"
4. Check your email and follow the link
5. Create a new password

## Using the Feed

### Creating a Post
1. Go to Feed (home icon)
2. Type your message in the text box (max 280 characters)
3. Use emojis from your keyboard if desired
4. Optional: Click "Add Photo" to attach an image
5. Optional: Check "Friends only" to limit who sees it
6. Click "Post"

### Commenting
1. Click "Comment" on a post
2. Type your comment
3. Click "Post"
4. Click "Comment" again to hide comments

### Downloading Images
- Click the "Download" link on posts with images
- Image will save to your downloads folder

### Adding Friends from Feed
- Click "Add Friend" on any post by someone you're not friends with
- They'll be added to your friends list

## Managing Your Profile

### Viewing Your Profile
1. Click "Profile" in the navigation
2. See your posts, gallery, and information

### Editing Profile
1. Go to your Profile
2. Click "Edit Profile"
3. Update your information:
   - Full Name
   - Age
   - City
   - Country
   - About me (max 500 characters)
   - Choose a banner style
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Changing Profile Picture
1. Go to your Profile
2. Click "Edit" on your profile picture
3. Select an image from your device
4. Wait for upload to complete

### Viewing Your Posts
1. Go to Profile
2. Click "Posts" tab
3. All your posts appear here

### Viewing Your Gallery
1. Go to Profile
2. Click "Gallery" tab
3. All images from your posts appear here
4. Click any image to view full size

## Friends

### Viewing Friends List
1. Click "Friends" in navigation
2. See all your friends with their info

### Adding Friends
Method 1 - From Search:
1. Click "Search" in navigation
2. Type a name or location
3. Click "Search"
4. Click "Add Friend" on anyone

Method 2 - From Feed:
1. See a post by someone
2. Click "Add Friend" button
3. They're added instantly

Method 3 - From Suggestions:
1. Go to Friends page
2. Scroll to "People You May Know"
3. Click "Add Friend" on suggestions

### Removing Friends
1. Go to Friends page
2. Find the friend you want to remove
3. Click "Remove"
4. Confirm when asked

### Viewing Friend Profiles
1. Click on any friend's name or avatar
2. Their profile opens in a modal
3. See their public information

## Messaging

### Starting a Conversation
1. Click "Messages" in navigation
2. Click on a friend's name in the left sidebar
3. Type your message in the box at the bottom
4. Click "Send"

### Reading Messages
1. Go to Messages
2. Click on a conversation
3. Messages appear in the center
4. Your messages are on the right (blue)
5. Their messages are on the left (gray)

### Important Note
- Messages are not instant - refresh to see new messages
- You can only message people on your friends list

## Search

### Searching for Users
1. Click "Search" in navigation
2. Type a name, city, or country
3. Click "Search"
4. Results appear below

### Public Search (Not Logged In)
1. On the welcome page, scroll down
2. Use the search box
3. View public profiles
4. Log in to add them as friends

## Settings

### Changing Theme
1. Click "Settings" in navigation
2. Under "Theme Settings", select a theme:
   - Default Blue
   - Dark Mode
   - Sunset Orange
   - Forest Green
   - Lavender Dreams
3. Theme changes immediately

### Updating Display Name
1. Go to Settings
2. Type new display name
3. Click "Update Display Name"
4. This is what others see as your name

### Referral Program

#### Generating Invite Codes
1. Go to Settings
2. Find "Referral Program" section
3. Click "Generate Invite Code"
4. Maximum 5 codes per month
5. Share code with friends

#### Checking Referral Points
- View your points in Settings under Referral Program
- You get 1 point per successful referral

#### Opting Out
1. Go to Settings
2. Click "Opt Out of Referral Program"
3. Confirm when asked
4. All your codes and points are deleted

### Managing Billing
1. Go to Settings
2. Under "Billing Information"
3. Enter billing name and address
4. Click "Update Billing Info"
5. Note: Actual payments handled externally

### Downloading Your Data
1. Go to Settings
2. Click "Download My Data"
3. A JSON file downloads with:
   - Your profile information
   - All your posts
   - Your data

### Deleting Your Account
1. Go to Settings
2. Scroll to "Danger Zone"
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Confirm by typing "DELETE"
5. Your account and all data are permanently removed

## Admin Features (Admin Users Only)

### Accessing Admin Page
- Click "Admin" in navigation (only visible to admins)

### Viewing Users
- See all registered users with their emails
- View username and registration details

### Managing Users
- Click "Make Admin" to promote a user
- Click "Delete" to remove a user account
- Use carefully - deletions are permanent

### Viewing Admins
- See all current admins
- Remove admin privileges with "Remove Admin"

### Moderating Posts
- See recent posts from all users
- Click "Delete" to remove inappropriate content
- Deleted posts cannot be recovered

## Owner Features (Owner Only)

### Accessing Owner Dashboard
- Click "Owner" in navigation (only visible to you)

### Viewing Statistics
- Total Users: All registered accounts
- Total Posts: All posts ever created
- Active Today: Users who logged in today

### Exporting Data
1. Go to Owner Dashboard
2. Click "Export All Data"
3. Downloads JSON file with:
   - All user data
   - All posts
   - Complete site backup

### Important Notes
- You never lose access even if you change email
- Your email is hardcoded as owner
- Keep your password secure

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I change my email?**
A: Yes, but you'll need to contact support to update authentication.

**Q: How do I become an admin?**
A: The owner or an existing admin must promote you.

**Q: Are my messages private?**
A: Yes, only you and the recipient can see them.

**Q: Can I post videos?**
A: Not currently - only images are supported.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: No, but the website works perfectly on mobile browsers.

**Q: What happens to my posts if I delete my account?**
A: All your posts and data are permanently deleted.

**Q: Can I block users?**
A: Not currently - you can remove them as friends.

**Q: How do I report inappropriate content?**
A: Contact an admin or the owner directly.

## Troubleshooting

### Can't log in
- Check email and password are correct
- Try "Forgot password" to reset
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser

### Images won't upload
- Check file size (must be under 10MB)
- Check format (JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP only)
- Check internet connection
- Try a different image

### Posts not showing
- Refresh the page
- Check if post is "Friends only"
- Log out and log back in
- Clear browser cache

### Messages not appearing
- Refresh the page
- Messages aren't instant
- Check you're in the right conversation
- Verify internet connection

### Theme not changing
- Try refreshing the page
- Check Settings saved properly
- Clear browser cache
- Try logging out and back in
